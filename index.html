About

My Journey so far.

  • 2009-2010

    My "Humble" Beginnings

    After completing my BBA, like most, I was a confused lad and I chose to break into the call-centre industry. Which wasn't too bad since it was a hardcore tele-sales profile and I was happy that I was the least costly and most target achieving person throughout the company. Unfortunately though, time doesn't wait for anybody and I knew when I had to move on. After that I joined Advertia, in a direct advertisement profile... So, I did that for a while.

  • 2010-2011

    A curiosity is Born

    I found myself on verge of being very curious about different languages there were to learn. Since I already knew English, Hindi and a little bit of Urdu & Arabic; I decided to dedicate one year learning beginner levels of French and Spanish. One of the best times I had while pursuing some education.

  • November 2011

    Learning never ends

    Curiosity to learn new languages soon turned into learning new cultures and expanding my knowledge on global front. In late November 2011 I decided to go and settle down in New Zealand for more specialized learning and implementation of marketing. There I would get awesome opportunities to work on projects I have mentioned before, B2B IT sales for Vodafone, B2B Power sales for Mercury Energy, etc. all. I found everybody to be very nice and friendly, But it was 3 and a half years down the line that I started relizing that I needed to apply the skills I gained, where I came from. However, Being in Auckland was one of the best times of my life.

  • July 2014

    Implementation

    Provided the skills I gained studying at AUT University, Auckland and experience I gained while working there was still to be tested in India, so I decided to see wether I can turn things around for a dwindling firm and Voila! Things did work out as I planned. Now, I am seeking opportunities where I can solely focus on my growth, give my best based on all I have learnt and build myself a future and a career.
